ICARE

ICARE is a badge that promotes empathy in software design, craftsmanship, and maintenance.

It's pretty simple, really.

Using this badge in a project is a gentle reminder to everyone involved that we should care about how our work affects other people. None of us live in a vacuum; we are all connected. Our work should reflect consciousness of that fact.
